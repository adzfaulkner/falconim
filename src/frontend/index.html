About

Falcon Interactive Media (FIM) have been providing Web Development consultants to clients in industries such as Fintech, Ecommerce, Broadcasting since 2016.

No task too small or too great as FIM has a proven track record of providing resource to a whole range of projects. From struggling start-ups to providing short term development resource to mature projects, FIM excels in delivering solutions in an efficient and goal driven manner.

We truly take pride in striving to deliver quality solutions to all clients in a past and future tense.

Services

FIM have been supplying consultants to primarily interactive media projects.

We pride ourselves in specialising in "all stack" web development. In that, FIM can help with providing full stack web / mobile resource as well as Devops. We can be utilised as the "swiss army knife" no matter how complex the project is.

Open source technologies is our forte. Our consultants are proficient in a whole range of web based languages from the frontend HTML/CSS/LESS/SASS/Javascript to backend focussed PHP/Perl/Go & several SQL dialects.

We have developed on platforms such as Cloud based services AWS, GCP and internal hosted applications. FIM has extensive experience in implementing/maintaining CI/CD pipelines as well building Docker containers for Serverless apps and cluster based architecture such as Kubernetes.
